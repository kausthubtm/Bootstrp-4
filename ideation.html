NITK-SHOP

An online shop exclusive to the students of National Institute of Karnataka Surathkal.

INTRODUCTION

This page is an online platform to buy, sell and rent items exclusively for the students studying at National Institute of Karnataka Surathkal (NITK). This shop would contain all the necessary items for a student studying in NITK ranging from stationary to laundry items. This makes life easier for the students as essential things realted to academics, and nitk are available with just a few clicks. Also, it saves the time of students searching for these things.

FEATURES

The features of this website are:
  • Buying items : the user can buy items just by adding it to the cart and completing the payment.
  • Selling items : the user can upload items to sell,along with images, price and a brief discription of the item.
  • renting items: the user can also rent items and return them back after the stipulated time.
